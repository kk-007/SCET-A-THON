About The Institute

Sarvajanik Education Society is an emblem of quality and value education for more than ten decades. Sarvajanik College of Engineering & Technology (SCET) takes pride in being part of this elite and noble organization serving for the betterment of the society and the nation by imparting education.

This prestigious institute has been engaged in imparting quality technical education in nine different functionally specialized streams of engineering namely Civil, Computer, Electrical, Mechanical, Electronics and Communication, Information Technology, Instrumentation and Control, Chemical and Textile Technology. The institute strives to impart highest level of quality technical education through course curriculum and various professional training programs in advanced areas.

About The Department

The Computer Engineering Department was started in 1995. It is running Bachelor of Computer Engineering with intake of 120 and Master of Computer Engineering with intake of 24 students per batch. The department imparts education to its students with the help of its state-of-the-art computing facilities and highly qualified teaching staff as well as supporting staff. The department continues the journey towards the goal of creating technical excellence which imparts the best knowledge and skills to students with the objective of producing high caliber engineering graduates. A sound theoretical and practical work prepares the students in wider area of computer engineering.

About SCET-A-THON

Sarvajanik College of Engineering and Technology is extremely glad to announce a coding competition which is a brainstorm for every students to implement their innovative technical ideas into reality, "SCET-A-THON 2018” which is organized by Computer Engineering Department under the banner of UPDATES 2k18.
SCET-A-THON is 12 hours coding competition in which you have to brainstorm innovative ideas and implements ideas into reality for making surrounding smart.
It is stated that “Let’s Make Surrounding Smart” so the SCET-A-THON is based on the theme Environment, Health Care and Education through which we make society and environment smart.
Hence, the team SCET-A-THON welcomes you all to the new journey to "Let’s Make Surrounding Smart", "SCET-A-THON 2018".

Rules

1. Team Size : 4 Members (Students).

2. All the members must be from same institute.

3. Participants shall accept full responsibility for their own work and devices.

4. No participant shall engage in any malicious/illegal activities. Participants will be immediately disqualified if they are found indulging in malpractice.

5. Participants shall work to follow professional standards, when available, that are most appropriate for the task at hand, departing from these only when ethically or technically justified.

6. Participants shall ensure adequate testing, debugging, and review of software and related documents before submission.

7. Participants shall not use software that is obtained or retained either illegally or unethically, includes using Teamviewer or similar software. Participants will be immediately disqualified if they are found using it.

8. Any malicious attempt will lead to disqualification of the team.

9. Duration of competition is 8 AM to 8 PM on 2nd October, 2018.

10. Participants are not allowed to exit the venue until the winner announcement.

11. Breakfast and lunch will be provided at (SCET College) venue.

12. Participants have to bear their own responsibility to safeguard their belongings during the event.

13. In case of any emergency or medical assistance, please contact to the organizers.

14. Third party frameworks and libraries may be used, but must be declared in the technical prototype demonstration.

15. Internet facility and power supply will be provided.

16. Periodically progress of the work will be evaluated.

Theme

Education
Education provides the platform to transform education in an environment where the best ideas can be incubated and implemented that benefits to campus, management, and the next generations of students and instructors community. Ideas can be implemented related to digital teaching and assessment, educational learning games, social media in education, issues related to education, parking, transportation and so on.

Environment
By the year 2030, the antagonistic per capita ecological effect of urban areas must be decreased. Unique consideration ought to be given to air quality and city and other waste administration. What's more, we need to beat a lot of difficulties for that. All the contamination of urban areas must be diminished. Emission of smokes from vehicles and diverse enterprises are taking the air pollution to the next level. Measures ought to be taken to keep the outflow level in charge. Sound pollution is another wonder which is currently contributing in decay of city condition. Sounds produced by vehicles are influencing kid, old people and patients antagonistically. A major number of enterprises including instant article of clothing processing plants are being built everywhere throughout the city. These all are in charge of natural pollution of cities. Waste dumping in each place ought to be ceased. Nationals ought to be motivated to utilize dustbins to dump waste.

Healthcare
As the healthcare technologies are advancing, there’s an increased engagement and consciousness of consumers when it comes to their health. In such a scenario, the demand for remote care is more encouraged than ever. However, the current healthcare ecosystems are not equipped with technologies that can improve patient care by updating them with real-time patient information and allowing them to take proactive measures for treatment. Even IoT solution for the healthcare sector allows hospitals to improve the care quality while focusing on overall expenditure reduction. The team can think of providing solutions for issues such as Disease management,Enhancing patient experience,Better management of drugsImproving treatment outcomes.
